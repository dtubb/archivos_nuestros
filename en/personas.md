        <h2 class="h3 mb-3">Six roles</h2>
        <ul class="mb-0">
          <li><strong>Creator / author</strong>: the person who produced the original material or source work.</li>
          <li><strong>Curator</strong>: the person who selects, orders, and presents a collection for publication.</li>
          <li><strong>Editor</strong>: the person who reviews, corrects, and prepares text or metadata for publication.</li>
          <li><strong>Transcriber</strong>: the person who turns a spoken or manuscript source into readable text.</li>
          <li><strong>Translator</strong>: the person who moves text from one language into another.</li>
          <li><strong>Contributor</strong>: the person who supports the project without a more specific role yet.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>
